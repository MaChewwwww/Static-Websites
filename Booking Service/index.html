Grab

  • Angkas is a leading motorcycle ride-hailing service in the Philippines, providing fast and affordable transportation solutions.
  • Motorcycle Rides: Book a motorcycle ride with Angkas for quick and convenient travel, especially during heavy traffic.
  • Delivery Services: Use Angkas for reliable delivery services, whether it’s documents, parcels, or food items.
  • Real-time Tracking: Track your ride in real-time and get updates on your rider’s location and estimated arrival time.
  • Safety Gear: Angkas provides safety gear, including helmets and hairnets, to ensure your safety during the ride.
  • Cashless Payment: Pay for your ride using various cashless options, including credit/debit cards and mobile wallets.
  • Professional Riders: Angkas riders are trained and certified to ensure a safe and efficient ride experience.
  • Insurance Coverage: Enjoy peace of mind with insurance coverage for every ride, ensuring safety and security for both rider and passenger.
